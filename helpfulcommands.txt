The term "viewport" refers to the
available display size to render
the contents of a page.
viewport meta tag allows you to
define this available size.

Furthermore, ensuring that your scripts are 
loaded last will ensure that once you invoke
Document Object Model (DOM) operations in your 
scripts, you can be sure that your page's 
elements have already been rendered.
